📊 Loan EMI Calculator

📐 Formula Used

Principal per month: \[ P_m = \frac{A}{T \times 12} \] Monthly EMI: \[ EMI_m = P_m + \Big( \big(A - (m-1) \times P_m\big) \times \frac{R}{12 \times 100} \Big) \] Where: - \( A \) = Loan Amount - \( T \) = Tenure in years - \( R \) = Annual Interest Rate - \( m \) = Month number
